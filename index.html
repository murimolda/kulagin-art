Sergei Kulagin

Photographer with many years of experience in the creative and fine-art filmphotography.

An active mastermind, participant and organizer of exhibitions and festivals of pinhole (lensless photography) held in Minsk.

Lecturer at "Photo Schools Mir Photo". Specializes in teaching techniques and technology of photography and practice in the field of film photography.
